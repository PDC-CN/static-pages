During phase 3 of Canton Fair, there are more than 40,000 oversea buyers from industries of costume, shoes, cases and bags.

As the highlight of Canton Fair, the show will be reported by more than 200 mainstream medias including CCTV, Sina, Tecent, People’s Daily, Guangdong TV, etc.
